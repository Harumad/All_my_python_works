About Us

Shaping Future Leaders

Tuba Senior High School is dedicated to providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for tertiary education and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide quality senior high education that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and moral integrity in every student.

Our Vision

To be a leading senior high school recognized for producing well-rounded graduates who excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society.

Welcome Message from the Headmaster

Welcome to Tuba Senior High School — a place where dreams are nurtured and futures are built.

At Tuba, we believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where academic excellence thrives alongside character development.

Our three specialized programs — General Arts, Business, and Home Economics — are designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in tertiary education and beyond.

I invite you to explore our website and discover the Tuba difference. Together, let us build a brighter future.

Mr. Samuel Asare Headmaster

Admissions

Join Tuba Senior High

Begin your journey towards academic excellence and personal growth. Our admissions process is straightforward and transparent.

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form or visit the school for a paper form.

2

Entrance Examination

Sit for the entrance exam in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

3

Interview

Attend a brief interview with the admissions committee.

4

Admission Decision

Receive your admission result within two weeks of the interview.

5

Enrollment

Complete registration, pay fees, and secure your place in your chosen program.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • BECE results slip
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school report (JHS)
  • Recommendation letter (optional)

For fee details and scholarship information, please contact the admissions office or download the fee schedule.
